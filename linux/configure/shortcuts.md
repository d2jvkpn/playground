### Shortcuts
---

#### 1. Ubuntu
- screen recorder: Shift + Ctrl + Alt + R
- gedit: Ctrl + Alt + G
- Move window to workspace 1/2/3/4: Ctr+Alt+1/2/3/4
- Switch to workspace 1/2/3/4: Alt+1/2/3/4

#### 2. MPV
- s: save screenshorts
- ] [: speed up, speed down
- m: mute yes/no
- p: pause
- q: quit
- i: show information
- , .: frame back/forward
- 9 0: volume down/ip
- j: switch subtitles
- shift + j: switch to the first subtitle
- v: show/hide subtutile
